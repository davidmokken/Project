Minor Programming

This site is made as a submission for the final project at the minor Programming at the University of Amsterdam. For the final project we had to choose a case and creates visualisations using javascript and d3. The case chosen for this project is the issue of immigrants in the Netherlands with a specific interest in immigrants and people from immigrants background in relation to criminality.
