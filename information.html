Visualisations

Map of the Netherlands

Main Function: Shows the total amount of immigrants.
Dropdown: A dropdown to select the different years. The dropdown also changes the barchart and the fillgauges.
Hoover function: Shows the amount of total people with immigrant background.
Color scale: The higher the amount the more blue the province gets.
Once clicked on a province the barchart, linechart and fillgauges change.
Label/Title: The title shows the province (or Nederland) which data is currently shown in the visualisations. It changes along with the other visualisations when a province is clicked.
Dataset that was used can be found here.

Barchart immigrant descents

Main Function: Shows the distribution of the different immigrant descents.
Radio button: Changes the barchart to a total amount or to relative distribution.
Total Amount: Western shows amount of people with Western immigrant descent. The rest of the bars are the other different backgrounds.
Relative: Shows Dutch background vs total migration background. Furthermore, it shows out of which the total migration background exists.
The Netherlands button: Once pressed on the Netherlands button. The barchart, linechart and fillgauges change to show the data for the Netherlands as a whole.
Hoover function: The hoover function shows the precise amount or percentage shown in the barchart.
Color scale: The higher the amount, the lighter the color red is.
Dataset that was used can be found here.

Fillgauges that show data concerning safety feeling

Main Function: Shows relevent information as simple numbers and percentages in regards to data concerning safety feeling.
All Fillgauges react to the dropdown and click function in the map.
Grade for safety in neighbourhood: Average grade given for the safety in a neighbourhood.
Crime has increased in the neighbourhood: Percentage of people who feel that crime has increased in the last year in their neighbourhood.
Feels unsafe: Percentage of people who feel unsafe in general.
Belief there is crime: Percentage of people who belief there is a lot of crime in the neighbourhood.
Dataset that was used can be found here.

Bar Chart that show registered Amount of Crimes over 5 years

Main Function: Shows amount of registered crimes over 5 years in a province.
Several different crimes were picked which were also hidden in the report mentionned in the case.
The linechart reacts to the province chosen/clicked in the map.
Dataset that was used can be found here.
